Best Movies according to Gayathri

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Nanban

This movie says one to follow their heart in any pursuit. It also has a good love line and romance. It always feels good after watching this movie.

Die hard 4

The actions and fights in this movie always had me at the edge of my seat. I loved how thrilling it was throughout the movie.

Ponniyin Selvan

It is a historic film which actually took place and was taken with reference to a book written on it. Watching this really makes me proud of our country's history.
